GALAKROND "He was the largest proto-dragon who had ever soared across Azeroth's skies. So mighty was the enormous creature that the beating of his wings could flatten entire forests." Galakrond was the largest proto-dragon of them all. With an insatiable hunger that actually drove him mad. Nothing could fill this beasts belly wich made him feast on his own felllow proto-drakes, slowly, turning him mad, almost corrupting his mind. Not long after he started to feast on his brethren he started growing necrotic limbs and dozens of old-god like eyes on his already afflicted form. _________________________________________________________________________________________

AMAN'THUL He was the first one to wake up, all alone, in search for the rest of his kin. Not knowing they existed but feeling that there has to be more out there. Aman'thul, the founding father of the pantheon, set out to discover the other nascent world-souls. Searching, jumping from world-soul to world-soul he slowly but surely awoken what would eventually become his siblings. Aman'thul and his siblings later on being known as The Pantheon, set out to stabilize the worlds. The sole purpose of the Pantheon was to bring order to the worlds. They even embedded huge machines that you can see in different zones in the game. ________________________________________________________________________________________

TYR During the battle against Galakrond Tyr suffered a heavy blow, even with the Dragon Aspects fighting alongside him. In a particular moment during the battle, the necrotic inflicted and corrupted Galakrond caught Tyr off-guard and bit off his iron hand, leaving an open wound. With all odds against him, Tyr luckily survived and replaced his lost hand with a newly forged one, one forged in pure silver. This newly forged mighty fist of pure silver would come to be a symbol and inspiration for personal sacrifice and justice. It is also where The Order of the Silver Hand took its name and symbol from. ________________________________________________________________________________________
